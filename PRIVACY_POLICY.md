# WE — The Water Experiment Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 2026
_Last updated: April 2026_
**Version:** 1.0.0

WE — The Water Experiment ("we," "our," or "us") is a completely offline daily ritual app for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Information We Collect

**WE — The Water Experiment does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

- No account or registration required.
- No analytics or crash reporting.
- No advertising SDKs.
- No internet connection required.

### Data Stored Locally
- Daily ritual logs and preferences are stored exclusively on your device.

## How We Use Your Information

All functionality runs completely offline on your device. No data is sent to any server.

## Storage and Retention

- All data is stored exclusively on your device.
- Data is retained until you delete entries or uninstall the app.

## Data Security

- Local data is protected by Android's built-in storage encryption.

## Permissions Used

WE — The Water Experiment requests no sensitive permissions. The app operates fully offline.

## Your Rights and Controls

- Delete any entry at any time within the app.
- Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.

## Children's Privacy

WE — The Water Experiment is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
