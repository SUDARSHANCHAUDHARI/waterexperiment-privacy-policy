WE — The Water Experiment

Privacy Policy · Last updated: April 2026

Overview

WE — The Water Experiment is a completely offline daily ritual app. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account or registration required
  • No analytics or crash reporting
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No internet connection required or used
  • No data is sent to any server

Data Stored Locally On Your Device

The following data is stored locally in a Room database on your device only:

  • Daily ritual completion logs
  • Water intake tracking (glasses logged per day)
  • Selected affirmation category preference
  • Reminder time and app theme preferences

This data is never transmitted off your device. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app.

Notifications

WE uses Android's WorkManager to schedule a local daily reminder notification. This is scheduled entirely on-device. No external service is involved.

Permissions

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — To send a local daily ritual reminder (Android 13+)
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — To reschedule the reminder after device restart

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
